<h3 class='post-title entry-title'>
<a href='https://foodwishes.blogspot.com/2015/11/gingersnap-cookies-hot-hot-sweet.html'>Gingersnap Cookies &#8211; Hot, Hot, Sweet</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content'>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><a href="//4.bp.blogspot.com/-byk0tjEcAig/Vjje2geIyTI/AAAAAAAARsI/qHT26eafZIY/s1600/IMG_8641.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="254" src="//4.bp.blogspot.com/-byk0tjEcAig/Vjje2geIyTI/AAAAAAAARsI/qHT26eafZIY/s320/IMG_8641.JPG" width="320"></a></span></span></div>
<span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">I always tell people I&#8217;m not a cookie eater, but I guess
that depends on the cookie, since when it comes to these gingersnaps, I can&#8217;t
get enough. As soon as these cool, I chain-eat them until they&#8217;re gone; but
that&#8217;s not my fault. I blame the amazing spicy-sweet flavor, and irresistible
texture.
</span></span><br />
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><br /></span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">Speaking of irresistible texture, I&#8217;ve got some homework for
you. You&#8217;re going to have to make batches of these to find out your own optimum
baking time. I know, it&#8217;s hard work, but you need to somehow find a way.&nbsp;</span></span><br />
<br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><a href="//3.bp.blogspot.com/-6ApJUW80vqw/VjjfDjpgn0I/AAAAAAAARsQ/Blgu1omqy8I/s1600/IMG_8575.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="clear: right; float: right; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-left: 1em;"><img border="0" height="225" src="//3.bp.blogspot.com/-6ApJUW80vqw/VjjfDjpgn0I/AAAAAAAARsQ/Blgu1omqy8I/s320/IMG_8575.JPG" width="320"></a></span></span></div>
<span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">Depending
on the exact size of your dough balls, and how crispy, or chewy, you want
these, the baking times will vary from 10 to 15 minutes. As far as ingredients go, the crystalized ginger is
technically optional, but not too hard to find (check the bulk section), and it
really gives the cookies that extra kick of ginger flavor and heat.&nbsp;</span></span><br />
<br />
<span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">Obviously,
you can also adjust the spices to suit your tastes, but don&#8217;t make these too
mild. They&#8217;re called &#8220;snaps&#8221; for reasons other than their texture. I really hope
you give these a try soon. Enjoy!</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><br /></span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/Fac_E67gUFI" width="560"></iframe></span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><br /></span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">Ingredients for about 50 to 60 cookies (depending on size):</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">2 cups plus 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">1/2 teaspoon fine salt</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">2 teaspoons ground ginger</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">1/4 teaspoon ground cloves</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">1/8 teaspoon cayenne</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">12 tablespoons room temp unsalted butter</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">2/3 cup white sugar</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">1/3 cup finely chopped candied ginger</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">1/4 cup molasses</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">1 large egg, beaten</span></span><br />
<span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">- Bake at 350 F. for 10 to 15 minutes, depending on desired texture. </span></span></div>
