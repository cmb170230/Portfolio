<h3 class='post-title entry-title'>
<a href='https://foodwishes.blogspot.com/2018/11/mini-buffalo-chicken-egg-rolls-no-wings.html'>Mini Buffalo Chicken Egg Rolls &#8211; No Wings Were Harmed in the Making of This Snack</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content'>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><a href="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-TlAThOycyfQ/XAHBEiMqlPI/AAAAAAAATZo/O6g0xOLTfBEyNZcYQWz0H_YUu3dyapjmgCLcBGAs/s1600/IMG_0268.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" data-original-height="382" data-original-width="540" height="226" src="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-TlAThOycyfQ/XAHBEiMqlPI/AAAAAAAATZo/O6g0xOLTfBEyNZcYQWz0H_YUu3dyapjmgCLcBGAs/s320/IMG_0268.JPG" width="320" /></a></span></span></div>
<span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">These mini buffalo chicken egg rolls remind me of the
time I ordered something called &#8220;boneless Buffalo wings,&#8221; many years ago. Like
all Western New Yorkers, I have a deep affection for this messy, but delicious
snack, and so I was very excited about being able to enjoy some without having
to deal with the wing bones. 
</span></span><br />
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><br /></span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">As I waited for my order, I felt sorry for whichever prep
cook had been given the tedious task of de-boning them. However, my sympathy
was unwarranted, since what they were calling &#8220;boneless buffalo wings&#8221; were
nothing more than strips of fried chicken breast, coated in the same sauce.
While happy for the prep cook, I was disappointed they weren&#8217;t wings.</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><br /></span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><a href="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-RoFjQo-im8E/XAHBTh2c7wI/AAAAAAAATZs/h2JsoGfUbCsppThg1f5uGFoJcdO_7SB_ACLcBGAs/s1600/IMG_0308.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="clear: right; float: right; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-left: 1em;"><img border="0" data-original-height="443" data-original-width="540" height="262" src="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-RoFjQo-im8E/XAHBTh2c7wI/AAAAAAAATZs/h2JsoGfUbCsppThg1f5uGFoJcdO_7SB_ACLcBGAs/s320/IMG_0308.JPG" width="320" /></a></span></span></div>
<span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">So, it&#8217;s a little bit ironic that all these years
later, I'm now using chicken breast to create a Buffalo wing alternative, but at least
I&#8217;m not trying to trick anybody. That aside, these really do have a very
similar flavor profile, and I should probably be more concerned with the people
who are going to be upset I&#8217;m calling these egg rolls.</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><br /></span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">What&#8217;s not going to upset anybody is just how great a party
snack these really do make, and not just because they&#8217;re delicious. Even sober
people make a mess eating actual buffalo chicken wings, but that&#8217;s not going to
be an issue here, thanks to their very user-friendly design. So, if you&#8217;re
planning a party soon, I really do hope you give these a try. Enjoy!</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><br /></span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><iframe allow="accelerometer; autoplay; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/owAmNCI64MI" width="560"></iframe></span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><br /></span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">Ingredients for 14 to 16 Mini Buffalo Chicken Egg Rolls:</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">8 ounces diced cooked chicken breast</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">1/3 cup diced celery</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">2 ounces blue cheese</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">2 ounces cream cheese</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">1 tablespoon Louisiana hot sauce</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">salt, freshly ground black pepper, and cayenne to taste </span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">14 to 16 square wonton wrappers</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">more Louisiana hot sauce for dipping</span></span></div>
