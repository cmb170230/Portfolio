<h3 class='post-title entry-title'>
<a href='https://foodwishes.blogspot.com/2018/12/thumbprint-cookies-great-grandma-mitzi.html'>Thumbprint Cookies &#8211; Great Grandma Mitzi to the Rescue!</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content'>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: &quot;verdana&quot; , sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><a href="https://4.bp.blogspot.com/-7oWQ2AbtS2Q/XBQ8iSEZzQI/AAAAAAAATa4/VD89tacEhzIHruZies5BiobulVrPCxsLACLcBGAs/s1600/IMG_1010.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" data-original-height="333" data-original-width="540" height="197" src="https://4.bp.blogspot.com/-7oWQ2AbtS2Q/XBQ8iSEZzQI/AAAAAAAATa4/VD89tacEhzIHruZies5BiobulVrPCxsLACLcBGAs/s320/IMG_1010.JPG" width="320" /></a></span></span></div>
<span style="font-family: &quot;verdana&quot; , sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">My mother, Pauline, made really nice thumbprint cookies, but
unfortunately I never wrote down her recipe, and so I was forced to go online to find one, so I could refresh my memory. I knew the ingredients, but unless you have
them in the right proportions, you won&#8217;t end up with that perfect
melt-in-your-mouth texture these iconic cookies are known for. 
</span></span><br />
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: &quot;verdana&quot; , sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><br /></span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: &quot;verdana&quot; , sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">Above and beyond the buttery mouthfeel, we also want a cookie that
has perfectly smooth edges, with none of the dreaded cracks that give away a
subpar recipe. So, I did an image search, and came across <a href="https://wickedgoodkitchen.com/grandmas-perfect-jam-thumbprint-cookies/">a recipe</a> that looked
very similar, and believe it or not, it was <a href="https://wickedgoodkitchen.com/grandmas-perfect-jam-thumbprint-cookies/">credited to a Great Grandma named &#8220;Mitzi.&#8221;</a>
Since my last name is Mitzewich, that pretty much sealed the deal.</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: &quot;verdana&quot; , sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><br /></span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: &quot;verdana&quot; , sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><a href="https://4.bp.blogspot.com/-27ssBQfaERQ/XBQ8pMMHeCI/AAAAAAAATa8/5jUZ23v0WVwsN5o3kAEG7Ivh1ZCbZM06gCLcBGAs/s1600/IMG_0883.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="clear: right; float: right; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-left: 1em;"><img border="0" data-original-height="382" data-original-width="540" height="226" src="https://4.bp.blogspot.com/-27ssBQfaERQ/XBQ8pMMHeCI/AAAAAAAATa8/5jUZ23v0WVwsN5o3kAEG7Ivh1ZCbZM06gCLcBGAs/s320/IMG_0883.JPG" width="320" /></a></span></span></div>
<span style="font-family: &quot;verdana&quot; , sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">I usually have to adapt recipes I steal online, but with
these, I didn&#8217;t need to change a thing. They really were perfect,
as advertised. So, sincere thanks to <a href="https://wickedgoodkitchen.com/grandmas-perfect-jam-thumbprint-cookies/"><span style="mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">Stacy from Wicked Good Kitchen</span></a>,
and her Great Grandma, Mitzi.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp; </span>My Mom
would&#8217;ve loved these, although she would&#8217;ve asked where the chopped walnuts
were, since she liked to add those to hers.&nbsp;</span></span><br />
<br />
<span style="font-family: &quot;verdana&quot; , sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">Speaking of family, if you&#8217;re
entertaining some of yours during the holidays, these beautiful cookies would make a lovely addition to your dessert table, which is why I really do hope you give
them a try soon. Enjoy!</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: &quot;verdana&quot; , sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><br /></span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: &quot;verdana&quot; , sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><iframe allow="accelerometer; autoplay; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/54uasXDGEt8" width="560"></iframe></span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: &quot;verdana&quot; , sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><br /></span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: &quot;verdana&quot; , sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">Ingredients to make 20 to 24 Thumbprint Cookies:</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: &quot;verdana&quot; , sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">1 stick (1/2 cup) soft unsalted butter</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: &quot;verdana&quot; , sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">1/3 cup powdered sugar (not sifted)</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: &quot;verdana&quot; , sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">1 large egg yolk</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: &quot;verdana&quot; , sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">1 teaspoon vanilla extract</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: &quot;verdana&quot; , sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">1/8 teaspoon almond extract</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: &quot;verdana&quot; , sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">1/4 teaspoon fine salt</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: &quot;verdana&quot; , sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour (5 ounces by weight)</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: &quot;verdana&quot; , sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">1/2 cup fruit jam to fill centers</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: &quot;verdana&quot; , sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><br /></span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: &quot;verdana&quot; , sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">- 325 F. for 15 minutes </span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: &quot;verdana&quot; , sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"><br /></span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: &quot;verdana&quot; , sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">For the icing:</span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: &quot;verdana&quot; , sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;">- Combine powdered sugar with a little milk or water, and
mix until you have a consistency that will hold its shape when piped.</span></span></div>
